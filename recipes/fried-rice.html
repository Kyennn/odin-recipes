Malaysian Fried Rice

Description:

Best variation of fried rice in the world, singaporean food complete garbage

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Cooking Oil
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Salt
  • Chives
  • Shrimp
  • Beans

Steps:

  1. Firstly, Heat up the pan and fill it with a layer of oil(better with a wok)
  2. Turn on the stove and let pan sit until the oil begins to sizzle
  3. Next, Throw in half a clove of diced garlic and a 1 whole chopped onion
  4. Wait until onion turns golden brown
  5. Throw in chopped beans and let it sit for 3 minutes
  6. Throw in the shrimp and chives and stir well
  7. Add rice in and stir evenly
  8. Add Oyster sauce and mix
  9. Fried rice is completed
